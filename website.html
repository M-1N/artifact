The investigation of a missing person

On Oct. 2, 2005, I stumbled upon a photo in the woods near a trail on Belle Isle, Richmond, VA. What I found in this photo was quite a shock, as it depicted strange objects in the sky. Coincidentally, when investigating the date of the photo, it seemed to have coincided with a missing person case that had occurred in the same area at the same time. Since then, I have been investigating this case that seemed to have happened under very strange and mysterious circumstances.
